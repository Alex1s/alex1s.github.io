A simple application that interpets AutoTouch ClashOfClans script and adds usefull comments for editing.
Following additional options are availible:

  • change the scripts orientation
  • chnage the scripts resolution
  • use tap(x, y) commands to make your script simpler and easier to read.
I hope it will help some people out there.

When recording scripts (superficial tips):

What you will have to do:
  • Fully zoom out the screen and fully swipe your screen to the top or the bottom.
  • Always fully swipe your troopbar to the left or right.
What you should do:
  • Don't deploy troops by holding down your finger(s).
  • Start your script with the enemy heroes on the same spot as when you were recording.
To compensate the sim-life differences:
  • Use Jump Spells instead of Wallbreakers whenever possible.
  • Don't use Minions to funnel your troops, try to use Archers instead.
  • Clear the the let or right before deploying your Wallbreaers and never deploy them directly on a wall.
